Site Plan for Bay Area eBiking Club

This is the outline for the design and development of the Bay Area eBiking Club project.

Site Name

Site Name: Bay Area eBiking Club

Reason for Choosing the Name:This name represents a club that is focused on electric biking in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for regional e-biking by listing providing trail and e-bike recommendation information along with a membership information form.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What is the best e-bike to get for mountainous areas?
  • Scenario 2: Where can I find contact information for the club's directors?
